Need to soak up some sun?

We have got you covered! We have compiled a list of the top 4 beach locations from the most visited tourist states around the US. Also, we can accommodate to any location you are interested in. Just send us your beach adventure of choice!

  1. California. Laguna Beach is known for its city and surfable waves. They have everything from hikable trails to hot beaches to downtown nightlife!

  2. Florida. Clearwater Beach is known for their white sand and crystal clear water, hence the name. Also, they are located along the gulf coast which brings beautiful marine life!

  3. Nevada. Sand Harbor Beach is different because its located on Lake Tahoe. It has the scenic view of the mountains while presenting you with crystal clear water.

  4. Texas. Boca Chica Beach is one of the most beautiful beaches and it's on the border of Mexico and the US with the Rio Grande river seperating the countries. This is one of Texas's best kept secrets!
